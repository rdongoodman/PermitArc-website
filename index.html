What devices does PermitArc run on?

Is PermitArc only for restaurants?

We lead with restaurant pain — but any storefront with permits and licenses can use PermitArc. Pick your business type when you add a location.

Does PermitArc file renewals for me?

No. We watch, remind, and help you understand what's due. You stay in control.
