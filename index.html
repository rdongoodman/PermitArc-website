Common questions

What counts toward my interactive AI pool?

Weekly Patrol, expiration tracking, and proactive vault review are included — they do not use your per-location AI pool. Chat, voice, and document scanning draw from the $6 × location/month pool. See full breakdown on pricing.

Is PermitArc only for restaurants?

We lead with restaurant pain because it's real — but any storefront with permits and licenses can use PermitArc. You pick your business type when you add a location.

Does PermitArc file renewals for me?

No. We watch, remind, and help you understand what's due. You stay in control — we make sure nothing slips through the cracks.
