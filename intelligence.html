How intelligence works in the app

Patrol, expiration radar, and proactive review are included every month — they never count against your interactive AI allowance.

AI chat, AI voice, and AI document scanning share a monthly allowance that grows with each storefront on your plan — two shops means twice the allowance, three means triple, and so on.

Included Expiration Radar — daily watch for permits, licenses, and certificates expiring in 30, 15, or 5 days. Email, in-app, and optional push. No extra charge per alert.

Weekly Regulatory Patrol

Intel Deck → Weekly Patrol · scoped to each shop's ZIP and business type

Each week, PermitArc scans official government sources for regulatory updates and law changes that may affect your kind of business in that area.

Your benefit: Licensing changes, new requirements, and compliance updates — with links to official sources when something verifiable is found. Quiet weeks when nothing new appears.

Included

Proactive compliance review

Intel Deck → Proactive Compliance · runs separately for each storefront

Cross-checks that shop's uploaded documents — and HQ master policies when you use Corporate Vault — for likely gaps and soft suggestions. Runs when enabled in Intel Deck and once enough documents are uploaded for that shop.

Your benefit: Up to three suggestions per shop per week. The more documents you scan and store for that location, the sharper the review becomes. Catch missing or overlooked items before a renewal or inspection surprise.

Your benefit: Ask what's in your file — grounded in your paperwork, not a generic checklist. The more you scan and store at that location, the smarter the answers get. AI voice input and read-aloud on supported devices use the same included allowance.
