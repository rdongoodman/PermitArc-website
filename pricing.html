Why this price?

A health permit lapse, expired GL certificate, or missed ABC renewal can trigger fines, insurance gaps, or a shutdown — often far more than $39–$139 per month. PermitArc is your always-on compliance folder: scan once, get warned before deadlines, and see whether regulatory requirements or law changes may affect your area each week.

Does AI allowance grow when I add locations?

Yes. Interactive AI — chat, voice, and document scanning — uses a monthly allowance that scales with each storefront on your plan. One location gets one allowance; two locations get double; three get triple; and so on. Patrol, expiration radar, and proactive review are included per location and do not draw from that allowance.
